FEATURES

IDEAVERSE PLATFORM

IdeaVerse helps smoothen the innovation capture process and supports seamless connectivity between the innovation funnel and the Intellectual Property portfolio development process. IdeaVerse platform gives IP admins and CXOs a birds-eye view of the innovation pipeline and take appropriate & timely actions by identifying the innovation leaders and laggers. Inventors can also utilize the platform to get real-time access to the status of their ideas, and conveniently track the progress right till the IP filing/rejection stage as well as monetization. It also enhances transparency for inventors as well as P&L leaders.
